# Deep learning-based age estimation of fish from otoliths

This thesis topic is connected to an ongoing research project on machine learning-supported age determination of Baltic cod from otolith microscopy images.
The work focuses on preparing and assessing otolith image data, developing supervised models for age prediction, and understanding how data quality affects reliable model evaluation.

More background is available in the [detailed project description](https://jmeischner.com/projects/otolith-age-determination-baltic-cod/).
